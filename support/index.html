Quick FAQ

  • How do I add the widget? Long-press your Home Screen → Edit → Add Widget → search “AI Widget Daily”.
  • Why isn’t my widget updating? iOS controls widget refresh timing. Opening the app occasionally can help trigger updates.
  • Do I need an account? No — AI Widget Daily does not require sign-up.
  • Do you collect personal info? No personal information is collected. The app may use anonymous device-based usage to operate and improve the experience.
